Graphic Designer

Remote (Worldwide) Freelance

About the Role

We’re looking for a talented Graphic Designer to create stunning visuals that bring our clients’ ideas to life. In this freelance role, you’ll design logos, marketing materials, social media graphics, and other creative assets. You’ll work remotely with flexible hours, so you can work from anywhere in the world.

Payment is provided in your preferred currency. Applicants must be 16 years or older. No CV needed—just a strong portfolio.

Responsibilities

  • Create logos, banners, social media posts, and print materials
  • Ensure all designs align with client branding and project goals
  • Collaborate remotely with our team for feedback and revisions
  • Deliver high-quality assets on time

Requirements

  • Strong portfolio showcasing graphic design skills and creativity
  • Proficiency with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, or similar tools
  • Ability to work independently and meet deadlines

Nice to Have

  • Experience with animation or motion graphics
  • Understanding of print production and digital formats

How to Apply

To apply for this position, please send your application to careers@laiusgroup.com following the format below. Make sure to include all the requested information to help us process your application efficiently.

Email Format:
Subject Line:
Graphic Designer Application
Body:
1. Full Name
2. Location/Timezone
3. Phone Number (optional)
4. Portfolio Link
5. LinkedIn Profile (optional)
6. How you found this position
7. Why you're interested in joining Laius Group (150 words max)
8. Your availability to start
Attachments:
- Your portfolio/CV (PDF format)

We review applications on a rolling basis and will contact qualified candidates for interviews.
